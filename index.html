About Us

Welcome to Nancy's-sort of-Cookbook.
I have created this webpage to act as a basic guide for those who do not know how to cook and need some simple ideas to get them started. Underneath you will find a selection of recipes that I have found and think will be helpful for those who are not sure where to start. Each recipe will include a list of all ingredients and the instructions. Whilst I myself like to venture out and try to cook all sorts of dishes, the ones down below are some I think are quite simple, but can be adjusted to your liking. So, if you are clueless and want some guidance, have a look and just try, not everything will be perfect first time.

Good luck and happy cooking!
